# Thumbnail Analysis: Notion Daily Planner

## Archetype: High-Contrast Split
Often uses a "Before vs After" or "Messy vs Clean" split to show the planner's value.

### Pros for UI Generation
- **Clarity:** Excellent for demonstrating "Organization".
- **Contrast:** The visual transition from "Chaos" to "Clean" is a powerful hook.

### Cons for UI Generation
- **Busi-ness:** Must ensure the "Clean" side actually looks usable and doesn't get cluttered.

### Suitability Rating: 9.5/10
Top-tier for productivity niches.
