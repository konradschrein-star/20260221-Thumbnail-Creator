# Script Analysis: Obsidian Tutorial

## Overview
A comprehensive "Beginner to Pro" guide for Obsidian, focusing on local storage and PKM (Personal Knowledge Management).

## Breakdown
1.  **Philosophy (0:00 - 1:30):** Explains local-first, markdown-based notes.
2.  **Setup (1:30 - 3:00):** Creating a vault and the first note.
3.  **Linking (3:00 - 5:00):** The power of Backlinks and the Graph View.
4.  **Plugins (5:00 - End):** Calendar, Daily Notes, and Community plugins.

## Content Rating & Quality
- **Information Density:** High. Comprehensive foundation for new users.
- **Structure:** Installation -> Core Concepts -> Customization.
- **Tone:** Calm and encouraging (Second Brain vibe).

The perfect onboarding guide for the Obsidian ecosystem.
